Who we are

A Prague-based technology studio for businesses that refuse to stand still.

Casus Belli is the technology brand of SVS DIGITAL s.r.o., a small, senior team of engineers, architects and security specialists working out of Prague 9. We collaborate with founders, CTOs and operations leaders across Czech Republic and the wider EU.

We don’t sell hype. We turn unclear technical problems into clear, budgeted roadmaps — and then execute them, end to end. From a single critical integration to a multi-year platform overhaul, our engagements are built on honest scoping, transparent reporting and code we’re proud to ship.

  • Senior-only delivery teams — no junior hand-offs
  • Fixed-scope discovery before any long-term commitment
  • Documentation, ownership and source code always handed over
  • Compliance-aware engineering for regulated industries

Our mission

We exist to give serious businesses a technology partner they can trust to do the unglamorous parts well.

Casus Belli was founded on the belief that most digital projects fail not because of bad ideas, but because of unclear scope, hidden trade-offs and teams that quietly disappear when things get hard. Our mission is to be the opposite of that experience.

We commit to the boring fundamentals — clear documentation, working backups, sensible monitoring, secure defaults — so that the ambitious parts of your roadmap have something solid to stand on. When we’re done, you keep the code, the architecture and the institutional knowledge.

Technology should compound, not interrupt, the value your business creates. That belief is the only reason this company exists.
